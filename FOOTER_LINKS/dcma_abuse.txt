2) Report Abuse or Misuse

If you believe the platform is being used for spam, harassment, unlawful activity, or other misuse, contact us at support@auctionintel.us
 with:

your name and contact info,

a description of the issue,

relevant URLs, screenshots, or examples,

the account email (if known).

We may investigate and take action, including warning, suspending, or terminating accounts.

3) Copyright (DMCA) Notices

If you believe content accessible through our Services infringes your copyright, send a notice to support@auctionintel.us
 with the subject line “DMCA Notice” and include:

identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed,

identification of the material and where it appears (URL and details),

your name, address, phone number, and email,

a statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized,

a statement under penalty of perjury that the notice is accurate and you are the owner or authorized to act,

your physical or electronic signature.

If you believe a DMCA notice was submitted in error, you may send a counter-notice to the same email with the required legal statements and identification.

4) Law Enforcement Requests

Law enforcement may submit requests to support@auctionintel.us
. We will respond as required by law and may require valid legal process.
