# Auction Intel Privacy Policy

When you create an account or contact us, we may collect:

* Name
* Email address
* Company name
* Phone number
* Login credentials (or authentication-related data)

### 1.2 Payment and Billing Information

When you add wallet funds or make payments, payment transactions may be processed by third-party payment processors (such as Stripe). We may receive transaction metadata such as:

* Payment status
* Payment amount
* Transaction identifiers
* Last 4 digits / card brand (if provided by processor)

### 1.3 Usage and Technical Information

We may collect:

* IP address
* Browser type and device information
* Pages visited
* Feature usage
* Search activity and platform interactions
* Timestamps and log data
* Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 7)

### 1.4 Inputs and Research Requests

We collect information you submit to use the Services, including:

* Nonprofit names
* Domains / event page links
* Search filters
* Research requests
* Export requests

### 1.5 Generated Results and Stored Data

We may store generated outputs/results, including lead records and associated metadata, for the retention period described in the product (for example, up to 180 days, where stated).
