# Auction Intel Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** 02/25/2026
**Last Updated:** 02/25/2025

This Refund Policy explains how refunds and wallet credits work for Auction Intel.

## 1. Wallet Credits Are Non-Refundable

All funds added to your Auction Intel wallet are **non-refundable**, except where required by applicable law.

This includes wallet top-ups used for:

* nonprofit research fees,
* lead fees,
* and other in-platform charges.

## 2. No Expiration of Wallet Credits

Wallet credits do **not** expire unless otherwise stated in writing by Auction Intel.

## 3. Research Fees Are Charged for Work Performed

Research fees are charged per nonprofit researched, whether or not a qualifying event lead is found. This is because Auction Intel performs compute- and web-research work for each nonprofit processed.

## 4. Lead Charges and Billability Rules

Lead charges are applied according to the tier(s) selected and the platformâ€™s billability rules shown in the product at the time of the search.

Where the product states a verified event page link is required for a billable lead, leads without a qualifying verified event page link are not charged as billable leads.

## 5. Mistaken Charges / Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged in error (for example, duplicate charging, technical malfunction, or a billing bug), contact support within **14 days** of the charge and include:

* your account email,
* date/time of charge,
* amount charged,
* search/job ID (if available),
* and a brief description of the issue.

We will review the request in good faith. If we confirm a billing error, we may issue a correction, wallet credit adjustment, or refund at our discretion (or as required by law).

## 6. Promotional Credits and Free Trial Credits

Promotional credits, trial credits, and bonus credits:

* are not redeemable for cash,
* are non-transferable,
* may expire if stated in the promotion terms,
* and may be revoked if issued in error or used in violation of the Terms.

## 7. Chargebacks

Before initiating a chargeback, please contact us so we can review and attempt to resolve the issue quickly. Fraudulent or abusive chargebacks may result in account suspension or termination.
