# Do Not Sell or Share My Information / Removal Request

**Effective Date:** 02/26/2025
**Last Updated:** 02/26/2025

Auction Intel respects privacy requests from individuals and nonprofit representatives.

If you believe your personal information appears in Auction Intel results, you may use this page to request that we:

* **Remove or suppress** your information from future outputs
* **Correct** inaccurate information
* **Opt out** of the sale or sharing of your personal information (where applicable)

## Who can submit a request

You may submit a request if you are:

* the person whose information appears in Auction Intel results, or
* an authorized representative of a nonprofit/organization requesting review of listed contact information

## Request types

Please choose one request type:

1. **Remove / Suppress My Information**
   Request that we remove or suppress your personal information from future platform outputs.

2. **Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information**
   Request that we stop selling or sharing your personal information, where applicable.

3. **Correct My Information**
   Request that we correct inaccurate personal information associated with you.

## Information we need to process your request

To help us locate and process your request, please provide:

* your name
* your email address (so we can respond)
* the information you want removed, corrected, or opted out (for example: name, email, phone number)
* the nonprofit/organization name (if applicable)
* the domain or event page link where the information is associated (if known)
* any relevant URL(s), screenshots, or examples (if available)

Please do **not** include sensitive information that is not necessary to process your request.

## Verification

We may need to verify your identity (or your authority to act for an organization) before processing certain requests. If we need additional information, we will contact you using the email address you provide.

## What happens after you submit a request

* We review the request and verify details as needed.
* We may remove, suppress, correct, or otherwise process the requested information in accordance with applicable law and our internal policies.
* We will respond to you at the email address provided.

## Important limitations

In some cases, we may retain limited information as required for:

* legal compliance
* security and fraud prevention
* billing and audit records
* internal recordkeeping

Backups may persist for a limited period before being overwritten.

## No discrimination for privacy requests

We will not deny service or provide a different level/quality of service solely because you submitted a privacy request, except as permitted by law and where the requested change affects our ability to provide certain features.
